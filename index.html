About Me

Hey there, I'm Avery Day! I'm a designer from Las Vegas and a current student at Arizona State University. My path to this creative field was brewed over six years as a barista, where I mastered the art of crafting perfect cups of coffee while learning the importance of patience, attention to detail, and active listening. These skills have become the cornerstone of my approach to design, allowing me to create digital experiences that genuinely resonate. Living in the vibrant city of Las Vegas has infused my work with a sense of boldness and innovation, while my Texan roots keep me grounded and connected to the diverse stories that shape our world. My goal is to bridge the gap between individual narratives and digital expression, ensuring every website and application I develop is unique and universal. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring my other passions: painting, sewing, traveling, and curating the perfect playlist. These creative outlets not only enrich my life but also inspire my work, allowing me to bring a unique perspective to every project.
